NEW YEAR'S RESOLUTION 2023

In my remaining years in junior high, my goal is to discover love and revel in the carefree folly that often accompanies adolescence. I aspire to embrace happiness and relish the simple joys of childhood before the inevitable transition into adulthood. It's a period where I intend to be a blissful, carefree kid, cherishing each moment with a lighthearted spirit. The objective is not only to find love but also to engage in the sheer delight of being young. he emphasis lies on experiencing joy and maintaining a sense of wonder before the responsibilities of adulthood set in.

NEW YEAR'S RESOLUTION 2024

My New Year's resolution is to cultivate a sense of peace while diligently investing effort into both my academic pursuits and career. Concurrently, I am committed to maintaining physical fitness and refining my personal style to achieve a new and improved physique. As I approach my 18th birthday next year, I recognize that the time for decisive action has arrived. There will be no backing down, and I am steadfastly committed to persevering without giving up on my aspirations.
